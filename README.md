## Instrucions to Play

- Click 'Get Started' button.
- Choose between Rock, Paper or Scissors by clicking in the icon.
- Rock beats Scissors.
- Scissors beats Paper.
- Paper beats Rock.
- First player to win 5 rounds is the winner.
- Play again!
